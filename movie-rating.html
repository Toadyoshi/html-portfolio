The Best TV Shows According to Nicole

My top 3 tv shows of all-time.

The 100

Love the premise and the characters and their relationships.

Manifest

Found the plot really well-written and interconnected. Again, love the characters and their relationships. Only gripe is the ending really.

Game of Thrones

I don't know how George managed to create such an immersive complicated world but he did. The characters are brillant. In terms of the world, it feels like an accurate portrayal.
